Mapping Respondent's Socioeconomic Factors and Child's Body Mass Indexes

Reporting the 2014/2015 Southeastern
Pennsylvania Household Health Survey

Map Notes:

Circle size is representative of the amount of respondents per ZIPCODE.

Some ZIP Codes have been excluded from the map as there were fewer than 5 respondents which led to misrepresented data for the entire ZIP Code.

There were a total of 1,156 Philadelphia respondents in the survey, although not every respondent answered all the questions.
